How playing sports can reduce stress:

TREVOR ADELINE / CAIA IMAGE / SCIENCE PHOTO LIBRARY

  • Distracts the Mind: Playing sports helps take your mind off worries by focusing on the game.
  • Improves Sleep: Regular exercise helps you sleep better, which reduces stress levels.
  • Boosts Confidence: Achieving goals in sports can increase self-esteem and reduce feelings of stress.
  • Teaches Stress Management: Sports often involve handling pressure, which helps you develop coping skills.
  • Encourages Routine: Playing sports regularly creates a healthy routine, which adds stability to your life.
